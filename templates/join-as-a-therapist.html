Requirements

To participate in the Talkspace network, practitioners are required to possess the following:

  • Highest clinical license in your state of residence, allowing independent clinical practice. Talkspace therapists only work with clients in the state(s) where they are licensed and allowed to practice independently.
  • Minimum of 3 years of direct clinical experience.
  • Professional malpractice liability insurance policy.
  • Submission of a fully completed, signed CAQH application.
  • Individual NPI number.
  • iOS device (iPhone 5S, iPad 4 (or newer)) with video camera.
  • Reliable internet connection.
